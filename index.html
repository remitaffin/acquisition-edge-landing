The problem

By the time a company lists with a broker, you're one of 20 bidders and prices get bid up.

Great operators disappear in crowded processes. Waiting for listings means you pay a premium and lose leverage.

Our solution

We detect exit signals 6-12 months before owners formally decide to sell.

What we detect

Signals that predict exits and protect downside.

Owner exit preparation

  • Stopped responding to reviews and calls
  • Business listed then removed from broker sites
  • Recently hired CFO/COO to professionalize

Financial distress

  • Tax liens and UCC filings stacking up
  • Complaints spiking with no response
  • Licensing delays and penalty fees

Business decline

  • Review velocity down 30%+
  • Ratings falling month over month
  • Ownership or domain changes creating drift

Quality indicators

  • 4.5★+ ratings with rising review counts
  • Clean licenses, accreditations, renewals
  • Hiring momentum and new locations
