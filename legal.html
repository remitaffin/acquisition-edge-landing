Legal

Terms, privacy, and compliance

Acquisition Edge is a business intelligence service built on public data and respectful of privacy and compliance expectations.

Terms of use

Acquisition Edge provides indicative signals and scores for research purposes. Decisions to pursue or acquire any business remain solely with the client. Data access is exclusive per state and industry and may be revoked if misused.

Privacy

We collect contact information submitted via forms to deliver reports and updates. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Data sources

Signals are derived from public datasets including licensing boards, state registries, lien filings, review platforms, and employment data. Proprietary scoring logic is confidential and may evolve.

Compliance

We respect robots.txt directives, applicable terms of service, and relevant state regulations for all monitored datasets. Clients are responsible for complying with outreach and solicitation regulations in their jurisdictions.
