Terms of use

Acquisition Edge provides reports and alerts for informational purposes. Clients are responsible for verifying details directly with targets and complying with applicable regulations.

We use public, permissionless data sources and aggregate them for scoring. Client contact details submitted through Tally are used solely to deliver reports and respond to requests.

We do not sell personal data. Data is stored in secure cloud environments with access restricted to essential personnel.
